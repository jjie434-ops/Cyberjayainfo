Last updated: 8 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how M Century ("we", "us") — operating this independent property marketing and enquiry page for Myra Cove, Cyberjaya — collects, uses, and protects your personal data, in line with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

When you submit the enquiry form or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number;

This site uses Google Ads conversion tracking and may use related Google analytics cookies to measure ad performance. These tools may set cookies or process device identifiers. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data. Your enquiry details are not displayed publicly. We may share information with the developer or appointed sales representative where needed to process your enquiry, and with service providers that help us operate this page.

5. Retention

We keep enquiry records only for as long as needed to follow up and for reasonable record-keeping, after which they are deleted.
