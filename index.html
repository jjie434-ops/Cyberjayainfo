The Concept

A calm residential enclave in Cyberjaya

Myra Cove blends modern city living with the ease of a lakeside setting. It is designed for families and professionals who want room to breathe, practical layouts, and everyday facilities close to home.

Come home to green spaces, lake views, and a guarded neighbourhood atmosphere while staying connected to Cyberjaya, Putrajaya, schools, hospitals, retail, and major highways.

Whether it is a dip in the pool, a session in the gym, or time outdoors with loved ones, Myra Cove is shaped around well-being and space for daily rituals.
